Gas Chromatograph Methods: Auxiliary Zones

  1. Select On or Off for AUX Thermal Zone #1. If On is selected, enter the desired temperature in the box to the right.
  2. Select On or Off for AUX Thermal Zone #2. If On is selected, enter the desired temperature in the box to the right.
  3. Select On or Off for Aux EPC #1.
  4. Select On or Off for Aux EPC #2.
  5. Select On or Off for Aux EPC #3.
  6. Select Configure PCM A to access Channel 1 and Channel 2 of Pneumatic Control Module A. Select On or Off for each channel.
