Acquisition Queue: Editing An Active Sequence

Edit the Acquisition Queue While Actively Acquiring Data

The acquisition queue can be edited while actively acquiring data, with the exception of the row that is currently acquiring. One caveat, after making any change to an active sequence, the acquisition queue will automatically enter a paused state. Thus the sequence will not continue after the current row is done acquiring unless the queue is toggled out of the paused state by pressing the play button .
