Applying a relative detector voltage will increase the sensitivity however, it will accelerate the aging of the detector. Review the Acquisition Configuration portion of the MS method section for more detailed information relative on the utilization of a relative detector voltage. It is required to include a Detector Measurement in the tuning procedure when a detector voltage offset is applied for the data processing to work correctly.
