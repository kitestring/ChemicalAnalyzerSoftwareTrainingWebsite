Mass Spectrometer Methods: Tune Compound Valve Schedule

Tune Compound Valve Schedule

  • The tune compound valve schedule controls when PFTBA is allowed to flow in to the source. The signals generated are used during instrument tuning and is enabled automatically regardless of the method settings. It is not recommend to allow PFTBA into the source when acquiring sample data as it will have an adverse impact on the sensitivity. Therefore, it is not necessary to enable it for normal instrument operation. It can be useful to create a diagnostic MS Method to verify that PFTBA ions are being seen by the instrument or for use when searching for leaks to provide a point of comparison.
